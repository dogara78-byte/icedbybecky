About ICED By Becky

ICED By Becky is a home-based bakery specializing in custom decorated sugar cookies and beautiful artisan cupcakes made fresh to order. Based in Brownsburg, IN, Becky brings over five years of experience and more than 1,200 happy customers who trust her for stunning, handcrafted sweets that make every occasion memorable.

Each cookie and cupcake is designed with care, creativity, and premium ingredients to match your theme, colors, and vision. From playful characters and elegant florals to colorful birthday treats, every order is edible art you’ll be proud to share.

Local pickup only.

How far in advance should I order?
  • Best to order at least 2 weeks in advance
  • Cookies typically require up to 3 days to complete
  • While last-minute orders are sometimes possible, advance notice ensures availability
How long do cookies stay fresh?
  • 1 week at room temperature (in packaging)
  • 2 weeks refrigerated
  • 2 months frozen
How long do cupcakes stay fresh?
  • 2–4 days at room temperature
  • Up to 2 months frozen

Do not refrigerate cupcakes, as they may dry out.

Allergen information

Our cookies and cupcakes contain eggs, milk, and wheat, and are made in a home kitchen. Please message Becky with any allergy questions before ordering.
